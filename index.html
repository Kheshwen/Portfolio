About Me

I'm Kheshwenda — known as Keshi — a second-year Computer Science student at Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS, based in Seri Iskandar, Perak. I build things that are both technically precise and thoughtfully designed, from system-level C++ to interactive game experiences and web interfaces.

I spent two years in Rotaract UTP in roles ranging from volunteer to Project Director, running events across media, PR and emcee work — which shaped how I think about communication, leadership and execution under pressure.

Outside of code, I'm into anime, action RPGs (Arknights: Endfield, Punishing: Gray Raven), stock trading, Indian cinema and cooking. I'm also actively learning Japanese and Mandarin Chinese — working toward full proficiency in both.
